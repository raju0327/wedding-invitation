He Said, She Said

We answered some fun questions about each other

Kishore's Answers

What was your first impression of Seetha?

"I thought she was absolutely stunning and carried this warm, gentle energy that made me feel instantly comfortable. I couldn't stop smiling."

Who said 'I love you' first?

"I definitely did! We were walking along the beach in the evening, and I just couldn't keep it inside anymore. It felt completely natural."

What is your favorite memory together?

"Our trip to Ooty. Getting caught in a sudden rain shower and drinking hot tea at a small roadside shop while looking at the foggy hills."

Seetha's Answers

What was your first impression of Kishore?

"He was incredibly polite, attentive, and had this wonderful, goofy laugh. I was instantly drawn to how genuine and kind he was."

Who said 'I love you' first?

"He did, but I responded immediately because I had been feeling the exact same way for weeks. I was just waiting for him to say it!"

What is your favorite memory together?

"The proposal evening. The amount of detail and care he put into setting up the fairy lights and vineyard walk was so romantic, I was completely blown away."
